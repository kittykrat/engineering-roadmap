# Kusto Detective Agency

## Digibus Real-Time Crisis

> The city of Digitown depends on its Digibus transit system, but a series of alarming issues are threatening the flow of daily life. From delayed buses disrupting commuters to fraudulent transactions in the newly launched DigiPay system, the city's transportation and economy are at risk. Using Microsoft Fabric's Real-Time Intelligence tools, your mission is to analyze the live and stored data streams, detect anomalies, and identify the root causes of the disruptions. Your investigation will reveal patterns and hidden truths that could restore order to the Digibus system and protect the city's future. Time is running out—it's up to you to solve the crisis.
